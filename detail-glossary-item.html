Net Revenue

Business Attributes
English Name Net Revenue
Chinese Name 净收入
Definition (EN) Total revenue generated from sales of beverages, food, and merchandise, after deducting discounts, promotional allowances, and product returns. This metric reflects the actual income received by the company from customer transactions.
Definition (CN) 饮料、食品和商品销售产生的总收入，扣除折扣、促销津贴和退货后的净额。该指标反映了公司从客户交易中获得的实际收入。
Type KPI — Financial Metric
Subject Area Sales
Is KPI ✔ Yes — Reported in quarterly earnings calls and annual reports
Business Formula
Net Revenue = Gross Revenue − Discount Amount − Return Amount − Allowance Amount
Unit CNY (Chinese Yuan, 元)
Related KPIs Gross Revenue Comp Store Sales Growth Average Ticket Size

Source System POS Transaction System → ODS Layer → DWD Layer (sbux_cdm)
Underlying Table
sbux_cdm.dwd_order_detail (Data Middle Platform · DWD Layer) 🔑 Apply Data Access
Columns Used
Column Name Type Description Role in Formula
gross_revenue DECIMAL(18,2) Pre-deduction revenue per order line Gross Revenue
discount_amount DECIMAL(18,2) Discount applied (coupon / member benefit) Discount Amount
return_amount DECIMAL(18,2) Monetary value of returned items Return Amount
allowance_amount DECIMAL(18,2) Promotional / operational allowances Allowance Amount
order_status VARCHAR(20) Order lifecycle status Filter: = 'completed'
transaction_date DATE Date of completed transaction Time dimension filter
Processing Logic
-- Net Revenue: aggregated from sbux_cdm DWD layer -- Partition: transaction_date (daily, T+1 refresh) SELECT store_id, transaction_date, SUM(gross_revenue) - SUM(discount_amount) - SUM(return_amount) - SUM(allowance_amount) AS net_revenue FROM sbux_cdm.dwd_order_detail WHERE order_status = 'completed' AND transaction_date BETWEEN @start_date AND @end_date GROUP BY store_id, transaction_date
