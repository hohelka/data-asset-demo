Net Revenue
净收入
Total revenue after deducting discounts, returns and allowances. Key quarterly earnings metric.
Gross Revenue − Discount − Return − Allowance
SC Sarah Chen
 L3
Comparable Store Sales Growth
可比门店销售增长
Year-over-year revenue growth for stores open at least 13 months. Reported in quarterly earnings calls.
(Current Period Net Revenue − Prior Period Net Revenue) ÷ Prior Period Net Revenue
SC Sarah Chen
 L3
Average Ticket Size
客单价
Average revenue per completed transaction.
Net Revenue ÷ Transaction Count
SC Sarah Chen
 L3
Transaction Count
交易笔数
Total completed customer transactions within a period. Excludes voided and pending orders.
COUNT(order_id) WHERE order_status = 'completed'
SC Sarah Chen
 L3
Gross Revenue
毛收入
Total revenue before deductions. Includes beverages, food, merchandise and license revenue.
SUM(gross_revenue) WHERE order_status = 'completed'
SC Sarah Chen

Discount Amount
折扣金额
Total monetary value of discounts applied, including promotional coupons and member benefits.
SUM(discount_amount)

Return Amount
退货金额
Total monetary value of product returns approved and processed within the period.
SUM(return_amount) WHERE order_status = 'refunded'

Percentage of total net revenue contributed by a single store or store group.
